File Name: Greedy Crab

  • Name: Eugine H. Krabs
  • Height: 7 inches
  • Weight: 5 ounces
  • Occupation: Owner of the Krusty Krab
  • Species: Red crab

Eugine H. Krabs, mostly known as Mr. Krabs, is a red crab who lives in a hollow anchor. He owns and manages The Krusty Krab. He is very greedy and selfish, willing to do anything as long as it earns him money.
